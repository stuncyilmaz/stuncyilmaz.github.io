Planned Tools
I will use Python and especially Pandas library for data processing. I am planning to minimize the data processing with javascript.

Planned Techniques
Stacked bar chart:

Yearly Number of incidences vs. Cancer Sites, averaged over 1999 â€“ 2011. Stacking is done with respect to sex. This chart will give a general overview on the cancer types.

Multi-time series Line Plot:

Percent of all cancer incidences vs. time. Different lines correspond to different races. This chart gives an overview of how cancer evolved over time and across races. Through interaction I will be plotting for each cancer type as well.

Bubble Chart:

x axis: age range, y-axis: the race. Color coded for sex. Radius proportional to number of incidences. Hover on: number of incidences for the given race-age combination and fraction of incidences for the race, as well as for the age range. This chart gives an overview on how a given cancer type is distributed over race and age.


Planned Interaction
Bar chart: Clicking on a bar will interact with the multi-time series plot and the bubble chart. Clicking on the bar will select a cancer type, and the time series chart and the bubble chart will be updated. The time series chart as well as the bubble chart will have 'detail on hover' functionality.
