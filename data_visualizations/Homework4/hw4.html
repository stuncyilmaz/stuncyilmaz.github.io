Data preprocessing


I obtained the data in R. Using Pandas in Python I processed the data individually for each panel in the dashboard. For example for the time series panel, I prepared a dataset having variables year and genre, and averaged the rating over the rating sources r1, r2, ..., r10.

Functionalities


Basic functionality
A prototype in tableau can be found here.

Moderate functionality
There are three panels with two methods, namely two bar charts and one time series line plot.

Advanced Functionality
Clicking a bar in the left chart filters the genre and only the information for this genre is shown in the time series and bar charts.

Functionality Discussion

When the page launches the left bar chart and the time series plot show aggregated information over all the genres. By clicking on a bar in the left bar chart filters the information for the desired genre. The left bar chart itself shows how many ratings there are in the data set for each genre.
