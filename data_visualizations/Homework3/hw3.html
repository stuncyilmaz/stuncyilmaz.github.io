Functionality Discussion

TMulti-Series Line Plot depicts people killed or seriously injured by car accidents in UK between 1969-1984 per month. The lines represent respectively the drivers, front seat passengers and rear seat passengers. When the mouse hovers over the time range, the exact y-values for each line is shown. We see that when the seat belt law was introduced in 1983 the overall rate of injuries by car accidents decreases.

100% Stacked Area Plot shows the same data as the line plot. We observe that the introduction of the seat belt law does not change the proportions between the drivers, fornt and back seat passengers. The plot can be moved along the x-axis.
