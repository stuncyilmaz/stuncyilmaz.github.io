Functionality Discussion

The scatter plot shows the life expectancy in years vs. income in US dollars for US states. The population is coded in the radius of the bubbles, and the colors represent the region. When the mosue hovers over a buble, the population and name of the state is shown.

The scatter matrix plot shows relations between high school graduation rate, murder rate, life expectancy and the income. Again the regions are color coded. The mouse can be brushed over a particular area to highlight.

Parallel coordinates plot shows the relations between high school graduation rate, murder rate, life expectancy and th eincome. Partricular regions can be brushed with the mouse and the order of the variables can be switched.
